## When to choose Perplexity

Choose Perplexity when your work depends on accurate, current information. Unlike static training-data agents, Perplexity searches the live web and cites every claim. This makes it the safest choice for research, journalism, due diligence, and any domain where facts change faster than model retraining cycles.

## What it does well

- **Cited answers.** Every response links to its sources. You can verify claims without trusting the model's memory. This is non-negotiable for professional research.
- **Deep Research mode.** Upload documents or specify a topic, and Perplexity builds a comprehensive report with sources, summaries, and structured findings. Competitive with dedicated research tools.
- **Real-time awareness.** Stock prices, sports scores, news events, and product launches appear in answers because Perplexity queries the web in real time.
- **Collections.** Save and organize research threads into shareable knowledge bases. Useful for ongoing projects, competitive analysis, and team research.
- **Clean reading experience.** The interface strips away search-result clutter and presents a single synthesized answer, not a list of links.

## Honest limitations

- **Not a coding agent.** Perplexity can explain code and suggest approaches, but it does not edit files, run terminals, or integrate with IDEs. For coding, use Cursor, Claude Code, or Aider.
- **Cloud-only.** Your queries go to Perplexity's servers. Not suitable for sensitive internal data, proprietary code, or air-gapped environments.
- **Citation depth varies.** Simple queries get solid sources. Complex technical questions may surface blog posts and forums rather than authoritative documentation.
- **Pro Search costs add up.** The free tier is generous but throttled. Heavy research workflows need the Pro subscription.

## Best fit

Researchers, analysts, journalists, students, and anyone whose primary need is accurate, current information rather than code generation or file manipulation.

## Pricing

- **Free:** Basic search, limited Pro Search queries, standard model access.
- **Pro ($20/mo):** Unlimited Pro Search, higher rate limits, GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 access, API credits.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for teams with admin controls, SSO, and usage analytics.
