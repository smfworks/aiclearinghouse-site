## When to choose ChatGPT

Choose ChatGPT when you want a familiar, general-purpose AI that handles a wide range of tasks without configuration. It is the default reference point for most users — the agent against which others are measured. If you are new to AI agents, start here to understand the baseline before exploring specialized tools.

## What it does well

- **Universal fluency.** ChatGPT handles creative writing, email drafts, code explanation, math, translation, and casual advice with consistent competence. No other agent matches its breadth.
- **Memory.** ChatGPT remembers facts and preferences across conversations (when enabled). This makes it feel more personal over time and reduces repetitive context-setting.
- **Custom GPTs.** Build and share specialized agents with custom instructions, knowledge files, and tool access. Useful for repeatable workflows like legal review, style checking, or customer support.
- **Voice mode.** The real-time voice conversation is the best in the industry for hands-free brainstorming, interview practice, and accessibility.
- **Canvas.** A dedicated workspace for long-form writing and code editing that separates from the chat thread. Better for documents that need revision and formatting.
- **Ecosystem.** Plugins, browse with Bing, Code Interpreter, and DALL-E integration mean one subscription covers search, coding, data analysis, and image generation.

## Honest limitations

- **Not a coding agent.** ChatGPT generates code but does not edit files, run terminals, or integrate with git. For production coding, use Claude Code, Cursor, or Aider.
- **Knowledge cutoff.** Even with browsing, core model knowledge has a cutoff date. Perplexity is better for real-time research.
- **Hallucinations.** ChatGPT is confident even when wrong. Always verify critical facts, especially in technical, legal, or medical domains.
- **Privacy.** Conversations are processed on OpenAI's servers. Not suitable for proprietary code, patient data, or classified information without Enterprise agreements.
- **Tool depth.** The code interpreter and browse mode are useful but shallow compared to dedicated agents. They are conveniences, not replacements.

## Best fit

General users, writers, marketers, students, and anyone who needs a versatile AI assistant without the complexity of IDE integration or local setup. The benchmark that other agents must exceed to justify switching.

## Pricing

- **Free:** GPT-4o-mini, limited GPT-4o access, throttled features.
- **Plus ($20/mo):** GPT-4o priority, higher limits, DALL-E, advanced voice, Canvas.
- **Pro ($200/mo):** o1-pro reasoning, higher rate limits, extended context.
- **Team/Enterprise:** Shared workspaces, admin controls, custom retention policies.
