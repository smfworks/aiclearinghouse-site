## When to choose Lovable

Choose Lovable when design quality matters as much as speed. It is the best prompt-to-app tool for founders who want a polished React/Node product without hiring a frontend engineer first.

## What it does well

- **Design-aware output.** Generated interfaces tend to look modern and cohesive out of the box, with sensible spacing, typography, and color choices.
- **Managed backend.** Lovable Cloud handles databases, auth, and deployment so you can stay focused on product rather than infrastructure.
- **Collaborative editing.** Multiple people can refine the same project in real time, which is rare in the prompt-to-app category.
- **Agentic refinement mode.** The agent can iterate on its own across multiple files, reducing the number of manual prompts needed.
- **Useful integrations.** Built-in connectors for Perplexity, ElevenLabs, Firecrawl, and others speed up common startup workflows.

## Honest limitations

- **Platform dependency.** The easiest path uses Lovable Cloud. Migrating away later requires exporting and re-wiring.
- **Limited model choice.** You do not pick the underlying models; Lovable routes them for you.
- **Cost at scale.** Collaboration, cloud hosting, and higher usage tiers add up once a project grows.

## Best fit

Non-technical founders, designers, and small teams who want a good-looking, working web app fast and are comfortable hosting on Lovable while they prove the concept.
