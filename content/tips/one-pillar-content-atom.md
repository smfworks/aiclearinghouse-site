# Extract Five Content Atoms From Every Pillar Post

## The principle

A Clearinghouse article is a content warehouse. Social is distribution of atoms, not a second research project.

## Why it matters

Teams burn out inventing daily posts. Repurposing multiplies reach while keeping claims consistent with the source piece.

## How to apply it

1. Mark 5–10 moments: stat, principle, story, checklist item, open question.
2. Write standalone captions (no "as above").
3. Adapt format per platform (thread vs short post vs video script).
4. Schedule across days; do not dump all atoms at once.

## Red flags

- Social posts that invent new claims not in the article
- Link-only posts with no atom

## Quick win

After your next long post, force a 20-minute atom extraction before closing the laptop.
