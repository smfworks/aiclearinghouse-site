# SMF Social Guardrails

## What it is

A policy skill that must load before generic marketing craft skills. It encodes SMF Works non-negotiables so SaaS-default playbooks cannot invent clients, packages, or polarizing hot takes.

## Hard rules

1. Research project / think tank — no services offers
2. Analytical AI callouts — not polarizing opinion bait
3. Microsoft-safe (founder employment constraint)
4. Every X post ends with the mandated CTA
5. Draft free; publish only after approval via Postiz or xurl

## Completion criterion

A draft is done only when the pre-publish checklist is green — not when the prose merely sounds good.
