# Weaviate Cloud — Free Tier

Weaviate is an open-source vector database with a managed cloud offering. Its free tier is genuinely useful for long-running prototypes, letting you host up to 100,000 vector objects without a credit card.

## What you get

- **100,000 objects.** Free forever on a single cluster.
- **1 GB memory.** Enough for many RAG and semantic search prototypes.
- **No credit card required.** Immediate setup.
- **Managed infrastructure.** Weaviate handles scaling, backups, and upgrades.
- **Hybrid search.** Combine vector, full-text, and keyword search in one query.

## Eligibility

- One free cluster per user.
- Available to anyone signing up for Weaviate Cloud.
- Upgrade to paid tiers as you scale.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Weaviate pricing](https://weaviate.io/pricing).
2. Sign up for a free Weaviate Cloud account.
3. Create a free sandbox cluster.
4. Connect via the Weaviate client or REST/GraphQL API.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Free tier capacity and retention limits apply.
- Paid tiers are required for production workloads, SLA guarantees, and higher object counts.
- Verify current limits directly with Weaviate.

## Best for

- RAG prototypes and proof-of-concept projects.
- Semantic search over document collections.
- Teams that want a managed vector database without self-hosting complexity.

## Verdict

Weaviate's free tier is one of the most generous in the vector database space and is a strong starting point for RAG prototypes. For production workloads, compare Pinecone, Qdrant, and self-hosted options based on scale and pricing.
