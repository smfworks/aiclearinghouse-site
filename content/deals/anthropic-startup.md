# Anthropic Startup Program

The Anthropic Startup Program provides Claude API credits and priority rate limits to early-stage companies building with Anthropic's models. It is one of the most straightforward ways to get substantial Claude API credits without a direct enterprise contract.

## What you get

- **API credits.** Commonly reported as $1,000 to $25,000+ in Claude API credits, depending on startup stage and fit.
- **Priority rate limits.** Higher throughput than standard API tiers, which matters for production workloads.
- **Community and resources.** Access to events, office hours, and technical guidance from Anthropic.
- **Safety guidance.** Anthropic emphasizes responsible deployment and can help teams implement safer AI workflows.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage startups building products or services on Claude.
- No referral required for the public application path.
- Higher credit tiers may require association with a partner VC, accelerator, or incubator.
- Review typically takes 7–21 days.

## How to apply

1. Visit the [Claude for Startups page](https://claude.com/programs/startups).
2. Submit your company details, product description, and intended Claude use case.
3. Wait for Anthropic's review and follow onboarding instructions if accepted.
4. Use credits for Claude, Claude Code, or other Anthropic API products.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Credit amounts and eligibility criteria change; verify directly with Anthropic.
- Credits are time-bound and non-transferable.
- Priority rate limits are subject to Anthropic's capacity and policies.

## Best for

- Startups whose product depends on Claude's reasoning, writing, or agentic capabilities.
- Teams that need higher rate limits than the standard developer tier provides.
- Founders who value Anthropic's safety and Constitutional AI approach.

## Verdict

The Anthropic Startup Program is the best on-ramp for startups betting on Claude. Credit amounts can be significant, and priority rate limits are a real production advantage. If your use case is customer-facing or high-stakes, Claude's safety profile is an additional reason to choose this program over cheaper alternatives.
