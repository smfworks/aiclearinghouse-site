# OpenAI for Startups

OpenAI for Startups is a program designed for founders building products on OpenAI's models. It offers API credits, access to a founder community, and resources for scaling. Unlike some competitors, OpenAI does not run one single flagship credit program; credits flow through multiple tracks including Ramp, researcher access, and invitation-only VC paths.

## What you get

- **API credits.** Startups can receive up to $2,500 or more in OpenAI API credits through the Ramp track, with researcher and VC-partner paths offering different amounts.
- **Founder community.** Access to events, office hours, and a network of builders using OpenAI.
- **Resources and support.** Technical guidance, product roadmap insights, and startup-specific content.
- **Model access.** Use credits for GPT-5, o3/o4-mini, DALL-E, Whisper, embeddings, and Codex.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage startups building products or services on OpenAI's API.
- Specific tracks have different requirements:
  - **Ramp:** Often for startups associated with partner organizations.
  - **Researcher Access:** For academic and research-driven projects.
  - **VC/Partner Paths:** Invitation-only and typically tied to investor relationships.

## How to apply

1. Visit [OpenAI for Startups](https://openai.com/startups/).
2. Choose the appropriate track for your startup.
3. Submit company details, use case, and expected API volume.
4. Wait for review and follow OpenAI's onboarding steps if accepted.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- OpenAI does not offer a permanent free tier; every API call is paid unless covered by credits.
- Credit amounts, tracks, and eligibility change frequently; verify directly with OpenAI.
- Credits are usually time-bound and non-transferable.

## Best for

- Startups whose core product depends on OpenAI models.
- Founders who want community access and potential go-to-market support.
- Teams that can qualify through a partner, investor, or research affiliation.

## Verdict

OpenAI for Startups is valuable if your product is built around GPT or OpenAI's developer tools. The credit amounts are smaller than some cloud or GPU programs, but the access to models and community can matter more than raw dollars. If you are model-agnostic, also compare Anthropic, Mistral, and Together AI startup programs.
