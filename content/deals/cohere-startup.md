# Cohere Startup Program

Cohere's startup program supports early-stage companies building with its command, embed, and rerank models. While Cohere does not always publish a fixed free-credit amount, it offers discounted pricing and program resources that can significantly lower API costs for startups.

## What you get

- **Discounted API pricing.** Often reported as 25% off for qualifying early-stage startups.
- **API credits or trials.** Program access may include trial credits or starter packages.
- **Enterprise-focused models.** Cohere specializes in retrieval-augmented generation, embeddings, and secure deployment options.
- **Cohere Labs access.** Some startups gain access to experimental models and features.
- **Program resources.** Technical guidance and startup-specific support.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage startups, often pre-Series B.
- Building products that fit Cohere's strengths in text generation, embeddings, or reranking.
- Application-based; terms vary.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Cohere](https://cohere.com/) and look for startup program information.
2. Submit an application with your company and use-case details.
3. Wait for Cohere's review and onboarding if accepted.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Discounts and credit terms change; verify directly with Cohere.
- Cohere is especially strong for enterprise and multilingual use cases; compare with OpenAI and Anthropic for general chat or reasoning workloads.

## Best for

- Startups building RAG, semantic search, or enterprise text-processing products.
- Teams that need secure deployment options or private cloud hosting.
- Founders optimizing API cost on embeddings and reranking.

## Verdict

Cohere's startup program is a good fit for teams leveraging Cohere's enterprise-grade models. The discount is meaningful, but it is not as generous in raw credit as some competitors. Use it if Cohere's model strengths align with your product, not just because of the discount.
