# Together AI Startup Accelerator

Together AI specializes in fast, cost-efficient inference for open-weight models and offers GPU clusters for training. Its startup accelerator provides credits and support for startups building on open-source AI models.

## What you get

- **GPU cluster credits.** Credits for training and fine-tuning on Together GPU clusters.
- **Inference credits.** Discounted or free inference for serving open-weight models.
- **Startup support.** Technical guidance, onboarding, and access to the Together AI team.
- **Model flexibility.** Use Llama, Qwen, Mistral, DeepSeek, and other open-weight models.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage startups building products on open-weight models.
- Preference for teams with meaningful inference or training needs.
- Application-based; terms vary by startup stage and use case.

## How to apply

1. Visit the [Together AI Startup Accelerator form](https://www.together.ai/forms/startups-program).
2. Submit company details, expected model usage, and training/inference plans.
3. Wait for Together AI's review and follow onboarding steps if accepted.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Credit amounts and eligible services change; verify directly with Together AI.
- Credits are usually tied to Together AI's platform and cannot be transferred.
- Training clusters and high-throughput inference may have separate approval processes.

## Best for

- Startups committed to open-weight models rather than proprietary APIs.
- Teams that need both training/fine-tuning infrastructure and serving endpoints.
- Founders optimizing for inference cost and model portability.

## Verdict

Together AI's startup program is ideal for teams that want to own their model stack. It combines training credits with discounted inference, which is a rare pairing. If your product relies on open weights, this is one of the most relevant programs available.
