# Lamini Free Trial

Lamini is an enterprise platform for fine-tuning, deploying, and running open-weight models with a focus on data privacy and model ownership. Their free trial lets teams evaluate the platform before committing.

## What you get

- **Trial credits.** Evaluate fine-tuning and inference workloads.
- **Private model hosting.** Keep models and data within your environment.
- **Fine-tuning tools.** Specialized workflows for enterprise domain adaptation.
- **Evaluation suite.** Built-in tools for measuring model quality and safety.

## Eligibility

- New Lamini customers.
- Teams with a specific fine-tuning or enterprise deployment use case.
- Enterprise tiers may require a sales conversation.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Lamini](https://www.lamini.ai/) and request a trial.
2. Describe your use case, data requirements, and expected model scale.
3. Work with Lamini's team to provision trial access.
