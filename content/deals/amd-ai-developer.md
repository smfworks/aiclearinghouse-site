# AMD AI Developer Program

The AMD AI Developer Program is a free program for anyone building, optimizing, or scaling AI on AMD hardware. Unlike many vendor programs that focus only on funded startups, AMD's program is open broadly — including researchers, students, open-source contributors, and ML engineers — and includes real cloud credits you can use immediately.

## What you get

- **Complimentary cloud credits.** Free credits for AMD Developer Cloud and Fireworks AI when you join.
- **AMD Developer Cloud access.** Test and optimize on AMD Instinct™ MI300X GPUs without owning hardware.
- **Direct access to AMD experts.** Private Discord channel and monthly "Ask the Experts" office hours.
- **Premium training.** Access to AMD AI Academy plus one complimentary month of DeepLearning.AI Pro.
- **Recognition and showcase opportunities.** Project spotlights on AMD social channels and recognition at developer events.
- **No AMD hardware required.** You can join and start building entirely in the cloud.

## Eligibility

- Open to AI researchers, ML engineers, students, open-source contributors, and developers of any stage.
- No funding requirement and no application fee.
- Available globally, with some perks (like sweepstakes) restricted to the U.S. and Canada.

## How to apply

1. Create a free AMD account at the [AMD AI Developer Program page](https://www.amd.com/en/developer/ai-dev-program.html).
2. Complete the program form and verify your email.
3. Access the AMD AI Developer Program Portal.
4. Join the member Discord and activate your cloud credit link.
5. If you do not already have an AMD Developer Cloud account, create one through the credit link to receive your credits.

## Partner cloud options

AMD works with cloud providers that offer AMD Instinct GPU instances. These are not all free, but they give you production-grade options beyond AMD's own developer cloud:

- **Crusoe Cloud** — AMD Instinct MI300X and MI355X instances with an energy-first cloud platform.
- **Vultr** — AMD MI355X Cloud GPU instances globally.
- **Hot Aisle** — NeoCloud with bare-metal and virtual MI300X instances.
- **AMD Developer Cloud** — The program's own cloud for testing and optimization.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Free credit amounts and eligible services change; verify directly with AMD.
- Cloud credits may be time-bound or usage-limited.
- Some benefits, like GPU sweepstakes, are region-restricted.

## Best for

- Teams that want to evaluate or deploy on AMD hardware without upfront investment.
- Open-source contributors and researchers optimizing models for ROCm.
- Developers who want an alternative to the NVIDIA CUDA ecosystem.
- Teams building privacy-sensitive or cost-sensitive workloads where AMD's price-per-VRAM is attractive.

## Verdict

The AMD AI Developer Program is one of the most accessible free GPU programs available. It is broader than NVIDIA Inception in eligibility and gives immediate cloud credits. For teams considering AMD Instinct + ROCm as an alternative to NVIDIA, this is the best on-ramp.
