# NVIDIA Inception for Startups

NVIDIA Inception is a free global program for AI startups. It is not a traditional accelerator with cohorts or deadlines; instead, it is an ongoing membership that gives startups access to NVIDIA's platform, training, hardware discounts, and a network of cloud partners. If you are building anything that touches AI infrastructure, models, robotics, or simulation, this should be one of the first programs you join.

## What you get

- **Free technical training.** Self-paced courses, discounted expert-led workshops, and full access to NVIDIA Developer Forums.
- **Developer tools and early access.** SDKs, frameworks, and member-only updates about new product releases.
- **Preferred pricing on NVIDIA hardware and software.** Includes discounts on select NVIDIA products and partner offers.
- **Partner cloud credits.** NVIDIA works with cloud providers like CoreWeave, Lambda, and Fluidstack; Inception members can unlock significant GPU compute credits through these partnerships.
- **Go-to-market support.** Exposure to investors, partners, and NVIDIA's global startup ecosystem.
- **No fees or equity.** The program is free and open to AI/ML startups at any funding stage.

## Eligibility

- Startups building products or services in AI, machine learning, data science, robotics, simulation, or related fields.
- No strict funding-stage requirement; pre-seed through growth-stage companies are accepted.
- No application fee, no cohort deadlines, and no equity taken.

## How to apply

1. Visit the [NVIDIA Inception application page](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/startups/).
2. Submit details about your business, product, and team.
3. NVIDIA reviews the application and follows up with onboarding instructions.
4. Once accepted, explore partner offers and update your profile to get personalized recommendations.

## Notable partner deals in the ecosystem

Inception membership unlocks or improves access to deals from NVIDIA-aligned cloud providers. Specific offers change frequently, but members commonly see benefits from:

- **CoreWeave** — AI-native cloud with NVIDIA HGX clusters.
- **Lambda** — GPU cloud and on-demand instances for training and inference.
- **Fluidstack** — GPU cloud credits for training and serving frontier models.
- **Nebius** — Cloud credits and early Blackwell access (partnered with NVIDIA).
- **NVIDIA DGX Cloud** — Access to NVIDIA's own AI training platform.

Check the Inception member portal for current partner offers, terms, and application steps.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Partner credits and discounts change; verify current terms directly with NVIDIA or the partner.
- Approval is not guaranteed; NVIDIA evaluates fit with the AI/ML ecosystem.
- Cloud credits are often conditional on usage limits, time windows, or minimum spend thresholds.

## Best for

- AI startups that need GPU compute, training infrastructure, or NVIDIA ecosystem credibility.
- Teams planning to use CUDA, NVIDIA NIM, Omniverse, Isaac, or related platforms.
- Founders who want go-to-market exposure without giving up equity.

## Verdict

NVIDIA Inception is one of the highest-value free programs for AI startups because of its training, hardware discounts, and partner cloud credits. It does not replace a dedicated cloud-credit program on its own, but it amplifies nearly every GPU compute deal on the market.
