# Google Cloud for Startups

Google Cloud for Startups is a tiered program that matches benefits to startup stage. The Scale tier offers up to $200,000 in Cloud credits, while a dedicated AI program provides additional support for startups building with Google's AI stack, including Gemini, Vertex AI, and TPUs.

## What you get

- **Cloud credits.** Up to $200,000 USD for early-stage funded startups in the Scale tier.
- **AI-specific support.** The Google Cloud AI startup program offers additional credits, dedicated support, and go-to-market resources for AI-focused companies.
- **Technical resources.** Training, architecture reviews, and access to Google Cloud engineers.
- **Ecosystem introductions.** Connections to investors, partners, and Google Cloud customers.
- **Gemini and Vertex AI access.** Credits can be used for Gemini APIs, Vertex AI model training, and TPU/GPU workloads.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage funded startups (typically Seed to Series A for the Scale tier).
- Associated with a Google for Startups partner (VC, accelerator, or incubator) for higher tiers.
- AI program has separate criteria focused on AI/ML product companies.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Google Cloud for Startups](https://cloud.google.com/startup).
2. Review the tier that matches your stage.
3. Apply through the partner network or directly if you are eligible for a self-serve tier.
4. For the AI program, visit the [Google Cloud AI startup program page](https://cloud.google.com/startup/ai).

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Credit amounts and partner requirements change; verify directly with Google Cloud.
- Higher tiers require an introduction from a partner organization.
- Credits are typically valid for one to two years and may be service-specific.

## Best for

- Startups building on Google Cloud, Vertex AI, BigQuery, or Kubernetes Engine.
- AI companies that want Gemini integration, TPU access, or Google Workspace embedding.
- Teams with a Google Cloud partner relationship.

## Verdict

Google Cloud for Startups is competitive with AWS Activate and Microsoft for Startups on credit size. Its AI program is particularly strong for teams that want Gemini, Vertex AI, and TPUs. Compare the three major cloud programs before choosing, because credits are often tied to the platform you will use for years.
