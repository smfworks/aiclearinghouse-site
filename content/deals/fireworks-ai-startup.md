# Fireworks AI Startup Program

Fireworks AI offers fast, production-grade inference for open-weight models like Llama, Qwen, Mixtral, and Stable Diffusion. Their startup program provides credits and discounted pricing for early-stage teams building on open models.

## What you get

- **Inference credits.** Startups can receive promotional credits for model serving on Fireworks.
- **Discounted throughput.** Reduced pricing for dedicated and serverless endpoints.
- **Fast cold starts.** Optimized inference engine for low-latency applications.
- **Model variety.** Mixtral, Llama, Qwen, Gemma, and many specialized models.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage startups with a working product or prototype.
- Teams planning to use open-weight models in production.
- Specific credit amounts vary by program tier and application.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Fireworks AI](https://fireworks.ai/) and create an account.
2. Navigate to the startup or pricing page and submit the program application.
3. Describe your use case, expected volume, and model needs.
4. Apply credits to your project once approved.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Credit amounts and eligibility change; verify directly with Fireworks AI.
- Credits are typically time-bound.
- Open-weight license terms apply to the models you serve.

## Best for

- Startups building on Llama, Qwen, or Mixtral who need fast inference.
- Teams that want open-weight flexibility without managing their own GPU clusters.
