# Mistral AI Startup Program

Mistral AI's startup program, sometimes called Mistralship, is a European-focused initiative that offers La Plateforme API credits and mentorship to selected early-stage companies. It is a strong option for startups that want frontier open and commercial models with EU data-residency options.

## What you get

- **API credits.** Approximately €30,000 in credits for Mistral's La Plateforme API.
- **Cohort mentorship.** Selected startups join a structured program with mentorship from Mistral and partners.
- **Model access.** Use credits across Mistral's model family, including open and commercial variants.
- **European focus.** Models and hosting options suited to EU data-residency and compliance requirements.

## Eligibility

- Early-stage AI startups, often with a European focus or EU data needs.
- Application-based and cohort-driven; deadlines apply.
- Mistralship is competitive and not open year-round in the same way as some other programs.

## How to apply

1. Visit [Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai/) and look for current startup program announcements.
2. Submit an application during an open cohort window.
3. Provide company details, product description, and expected API usage.
4. Wait for selection and onboarding if accepted.

## Caveats

- SMF Clearinghouse does not administer this offer.
- Program windows, credit amounts, and terms change; verify directly with Mistral.
- Cohort programs have deadlines and are more selective than self-serve credit programs.
- Credit use is typically restricted to Mistral's own API platform.

## Best for

- Startups targeting European markets or needing EU data residency.
- Teams that want an alternative to OpenAI and Anthropic with strong open-weight options.
- Founders who value cohort-based mentorship and partner introductions.

## Verdict

Mistralship is smaller and more selective than the big U.S. programs, but it is one of the best options for EU-focused AI startups. The combination of credits, mentorship, and European data options makes it distinctive. If you are not EU-focused, compare OpenAI, Anthropic, or Together AI programs first.
