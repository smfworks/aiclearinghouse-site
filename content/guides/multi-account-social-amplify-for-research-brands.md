# Multi-Account Social Amplify for Research Brands

Research brands often have one high-reach founder account and smaller specialist accounts. Amplify can help distribution — or it can look like a bot ring.

## Goals

- Increase discovery of long-form research
- Keep topic embeddings (for example X SimClusters) aligned with AI research
- Preserve trust: analytical voice, no fake engagement

## A clean amplify pattern

| Time | Account | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| T0 | Founder / primary | Long analytical post + link + media |
| T0+15–30m | Specialist A | Quote or reply with a different angle |
| T0+45–60m | Specialist B | Builder takeaway, not a duplicate caption |

Rules:

- One meaningful interaction per supporting account, not a pile-on
- Different angles, not copy-paste
- Same factual claims as the source article
- Mandatory brand CTA only where policy requires it

## What not to do

- All accounts reply to the same stranger within one minute
- Invent offers or client wins
- Polarizing bait that pollutes topic embeddings
- Auto-publish without approval

## Measurement

Track:

- Profile clicks and follows on primary
- Link clicks to Clearinghouse / repo
- Whether supporting accounts gain relevant AI-topic followers (slow metric)

## Bottom line

Amplify is a distribution harness. Keep it boring, factual, and staggered.
